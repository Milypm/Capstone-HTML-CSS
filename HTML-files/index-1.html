Program

Solo Artist

Damien Rice will share his experience as a solo artist. Live performance.

Creative Process

Starts July 16th @17:00hrs
Alice Phoebe Lou is going to talk about the creative process for a young musician. Live performance.

Industry

Starts July 16th @20:00hrs

Artist Life

Featured Artists

Damien Rice

Irish singer-songwriter, musician, and record producer

Began his musical career in the early 90's, owns three studio albums, and his live performances are truly acclaimed.

Alice Phoebe Lou

South African singer-songwriter

Began her career in 2013, owns three studio albums, and has caught the attention of the critics because of her singing style.

Tori Amos

American singer-songwriter and pianist, with a mezzo-soprano vocal range

Began her career in the late 70's, and owns fifteen studio albums.

Cantus Domus

Mixed Berlin choir, Director Ralf Sochaczewsky

Founded in 1996, has grown to over one hundred members, their musical work is often performed with unusual dramaturgy.

Lisa Hannigan

Irish musician, singer, composer, and voice actress

Began her solo career in 2007, has released three studio albums, and has received various award nominations in Ireland and in the U.S.

Alanis Morissette

Canadian-American singer, songwriter, record producer, and actress

Well known for her mezzo-soprano voice, she began her career in the early 90's, and owns ten studio albums.
