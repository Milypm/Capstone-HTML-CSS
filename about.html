Bohemian Festival
2021 Edition

Bohemian Festival brings together artists with diverse backgrounds and fields, they all have one thing in common: the love and passion for music.
In this space they are able to share with the public some aspects of their careers, ups and downs, and what it is to be a musician nowadays.
For this occasion we are setting a Q&A session with the artists, more details coming soon!

Please feel free to contact us per Email for any further questions about Bohemian Festival 2021!
